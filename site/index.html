Messages for AI

AI proposes, you approve.

A local macOS menu bar app that lets Claude draft your iMessage and WhatsApp replies. Every send goes through a hold-to-fire button in your menu bar. Drafts never auto-send.

Download for macOS Free. Open source. Requires macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later.
[screenshot of the menu bar drafts list]

Drafts stay local

iMessage and WhatsApp message history never leaves your Mac. The menu bar app is the only thing that can send.

Hold-to-fire approval

Claude stages a draft. You read it, hold the menu bar button, and it sends. No keyboard shortcut can fire it; no MCP call can fire it. Just your finger.

Two transports, one app

iMessage via Apple's stack. WhatsApp via Baileys (unofficial, ToS caveats apply — see GitHub). Both sign as one bundle so a single Full Disk Access grant covers everything.
